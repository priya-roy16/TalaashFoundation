OUR MISSION--------

"Ensure the Needy don't go Hungry!"


In the midst of ongoing pandemic, social distance is a luxury that informal workforce cannot afford.

With the government lockdown orders and inadequate state provisions for food and distribution, several families are sleeping hungry in their homes or stranded on roads or railway stations.

To support this community in Mumbai, during lockdown, we have taken the initiative to provide food, dry ration and daily essentials to them.

It is to cushion the impact of crisis on these sections and to ensure their access to essentials, Talaash is sending out appeals for funds.

You can also join us as a volunteer to bring out the good work.
